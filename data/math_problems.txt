Q: If x + 5 = 12, what is x?
A: If x + 5 = 12, then x = 12 - 5 = 7

Q: What is 3 + 4 × 2?
A: 3 + 4 × 2 = 3 + 8 = 11 (multiplication is performed before addition)

Q: What is (3 + 4) × 2?
A: (3 + 4) × 2 = 7 × 2 = 14 (operations in parentheses are performed first)

Q: What is 2 to the power of 8?
A: 2 to the power of 8 = 2⁸ = 256

Q: What is the area of a rectangle with length 8 and width 5?
A: The area of a rectangle is length × width = 8 × 5 = 40 square units

Q: What is the perimeter of a square with sides of length 6?
A: The perimeter of a square is 4 × side length = 4 × 6 = 24 units

Q: If a car travels at 60 miles per hour, how far will it travel in 2.5 hours?
A: Distance = speed × time = 60 miles/hour × 2.5 hours = 150 miles

Q: What is 30% of 200?
A: 30% of 200 = 0.3 × 200 = 60

Q: If 3x - 7 = 14, what is x?
A: 3x - 7 = 14
3x = 14 + 7
3x = 21
x = 21 ÷ 3 = 7

Q: What is the average of 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30?
A: Average = sum of values ÷ number of values = (10 + 15 + 20 + 25 + 30) ÷ 5 = 100 ÷ 5 = 20

Q: If a product costs $24 and is discounted by 15%, what is the sale price?
A: Sale price = original price - discount
Discount = 15% of $24 = 0.15 × $24 = $3.60
Sale price = $24 - $3.60 = $20.40

Q: What is 7 cubed?
A: 7 cubed = 7³ = 7 × 7 × 7 = 343

Q: What is the area of a circle with radius 5?
A: The area of a circle is π × r² = π × 5² = π × 25 ≈ 78.54 square units

Q: What is 8 + 12 - 5 × 2?
A: 8 + 12 - 5 × 2 = 8 + 12 - 10 = 20 - 10 = 10 (multiplication is performed before addition and subtraction)

Q: What is 3/5 of 45?
A: 3/5 of 45 = 3/5 × 45 = 3 × 9 = 27

Q: If a triangle has angles measuring 30° and 60°, what is the measure of the third angle?
A: The sum of angles in a triangle is 180°
Third angle = 180° - 30° - 60° = 180° - 90° = 90°

Q: What is the value of 4! (4 factorial)?
A: 4! = 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 24

Q: If 5 shirts cost $75, how much do 8 shirts cost?
A: Cost per shirt = $75 ÷ 5 = $15
Cost of 8 shirts = 8 × $15 = $120

Q: What is 0.25 as a fraction in lowest terms?
A: 0.25 = 25/100 = 1/4

Q: What is 0.75 as a percentage?
